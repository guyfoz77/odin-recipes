BBQ chicken wedges recipe

A delicious recipe of shredded chicken, black beans and cheese on a foundation of crispy wedges.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh chunks
  • BBQ sauce
  • Potato cut into wedges
  • Cider vinigar
  • Black beans
  • Water

Steps

  1. Chop the potato and cook the wedges as you normally would.
  2. in a pan, add the bbq sauce, cider vinigar, water and chunks of chicken.
  3. Whilst the chicken cooks, take the black beans and mash half of them with the back of a fork.
  4. When the chicken is cooked, remove it from the sauce and crank the heat up. Add the black beans and reduce the sauce to a goopy consistency
  5. Whilst the sauce reduces, roughly chop the chicken.
  6. Add the chicken back into the sauce, top the wedges with the mixture and put the grated cheese on top.
  7. Cook in the oven until the cheese is melted.
  8. Enjoy!
