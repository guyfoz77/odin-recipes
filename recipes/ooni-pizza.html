Ooni Pizza

A delicious pizza recipe made with my secret pizza sauce. People have said it is the best pizza they have ever tasted!!

Ingredients

  • Pre made dough, stretched into pizza shape
  • That amazing pizza sauce you make (secret recipe!)
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Misc toppings

Steps

  1. Spread the sauce onto the pizza base.
  2. Add cheese and toppings.
  3. Cook in the ooni for 60 seconds, rotating half way through.
