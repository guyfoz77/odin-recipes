Buffalo Chicken Wings

Some of the best wings I make. Simple but delicious if cooked right.

Ingredients

  • Raw chicken wings, separated into the two parts
  • Franks redhot sauce
  • Melted butter

Steps

  1. Cook the wings in the pressure cooker on high for 5 mins.
  2. Mix the redhot sauce with the butter and apply 2/3 to the wings.
  3. Cook on airfry mode for 20 mins, halfway though brushing the wings with more of the redhot mixture.
